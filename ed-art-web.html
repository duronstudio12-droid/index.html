Professional Separation
Services

CMYK Separation

Standard four-color process for photorealistic images on light garments. We ensure perfect tonal balance and sharp details for high-volume printing.

Spot Color

Solid colors for vector artwork. Ideal for logos and text-heavy designs. We provide precise Pantone matching for brand consistency.

Index Color

Pixel-perfect separation using a limited palette of square pixels. Great for complex, multi-colored designs on dark fabrics.

Simulated Process

The industry standard for printing photo-realistic images on dark shirts using spot colors. High vibrancy and soft hand feel.
