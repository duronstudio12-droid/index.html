Our Services

CMYK Separation

Full-color process separation for photorealistic prints on light garments. We optimize cyan, magenta, yellow, and black channels for vibrant results.

Spot Color

Ideal for logos and vector art. We separate solid colors precisely, ensuring exact Pantone matching and crisp edges for high-quality branding.

Simulated Process

Advanced separation technique for complex, photorealistic images on dark garments. Utilizing halftones to blend spot colors effectively.

Index Color

Pixel-based separation using a limited color palette. Perfect for detailed designs without halftones, offering a unique, stochastic look.

Behind the Studio

Duron Studio is a premier graphic design and color separation facility dedicated to the screen printing industry. With years of experience in both design and print production, we bridge the gap between digital art and physical print.

We understand the nuances of mesh counts, squeegee pressure, and ink opacity. This technical knowledge allows us to create separations that not only look good on screen but print effortlessly on the press.
